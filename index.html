Interesting Facts about Chameleons

Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder

Changing skin color is an important part of communication for them

Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to climb trees

Interesting Facts about Pigeons

Pigeons are incredibly smart and complex

Pigeons are known for their outstanding navigation skills

Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities
